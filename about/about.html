About Me

For the past 2 years, I've been developing video games for the desktop using mostly **Unity**. I do this because love what I do as every day there is something new and exciting to learn. In my spare time, the web development community is a big part of my life. Whether managing online programming groups and blogs or participating in technical events, I find keeping involved helps me stay up to date. This is also my chance to give back to the community that helped me get started, a place I am proud to be part of. Besides programming I love spending time with friends and family and can often be found together going out catching the latest movie, staying in playing games on the sofa or planning a trip to someplace I've never been before. I'm presently in Christ College Of Engineering where i do my **B.tech degree in Computer Science**. When not at College I'm usually working on a new project from home which could be anything to updating one of my existing application. I spend a lot of time learning new techniques and actively help other people learn game development through a variety of help groups.
